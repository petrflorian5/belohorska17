Getting here

Connected, yet calm.

Bělohorská 17 sits on the Marjánka tram line in Břevnov — well outside the congestion of the centre, but a short, scenic ride from it.

  • 1 minMarjánka tram stopDirectly outside the building on Bělohorská
  • 22 · 25Tram lines at the doorLine 22 — Prague's 'Castle tram' — runs direct to Pražský hrad and Malá Strana
  • ~10 minMetro Hradčanská (line A)A short tram ride to the metro network
  • ~15 minCity centreMalá Strana and the historic core by tram
